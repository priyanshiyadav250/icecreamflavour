BUTTERSCOTCH

Butterscotch ice cream, for some reason, just tastes like home. Maybe perhaps it feels more homemade than some other super sugary flavors, but the browned butter, caramelized sugar, and molasses in this flavor just feel like sitting on the front porch during a summertime rain storm. Only 16 percent of the U.S. say they like it, but we think it needs a resurgence.
