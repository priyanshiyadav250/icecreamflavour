BANANA FLAVOURED ICECREAM

Banana Ice Cream is a four-star Dessert Meal which can be made at a Cooking Station. It can be cooked by combining Slush Ice, Banana, Milk, and Sugarcane. Once collected it will be added to the Meals Collection. Meals can be placed in the world by highlighting them inside the Inventory window and selecting Drop. After a meal has been removed from inventory it can be positioned using furniture placement mode.
